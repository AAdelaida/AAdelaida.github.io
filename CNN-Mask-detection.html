Convolutional Neural Netword (CNN)
Face mask detection

Tensorflow

Código

This project uses a Deep Neural Network, more specifically a Convolutional Neural Network for a binary classification problem: differentiate between images of people with masks and without masks.
The training of neural networks requires a very high computational cost, in this case I used Google Colab.
The dataset used consists of 7553 images belonging to two classes: 3725 images with mask, 3828 images without mask, and can be downloaded from Kaggle.
